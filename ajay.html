SEO & Google My Business Optimization

A complete SEO and GMB optimization project for my Digital Marketing Portfolio website to improve search visibility, organic reach, and local presence.

Project Overview

I optimized my personal Digital Marketing Portfolio website to enhance its SEO performance and strengthen local presence via Google My Business. The goal was to make the portfolio more discoverable, attract organic traffic, and increase engagement through improved visibility in search results.

Objectives

  • Improve keyword ranking for digital marketing-related terms
  • Enhance local search presence via GMB optimization
  • Increase click-through rate and traffic from Google

My Role

Digital Marketing Strategist & SEO Specialist
I handled keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, and Google My Business setup.

Key Tasks Performed

  • On-Page SEO: Keyword research, meta tags, image optimization, and speed improvement.
  • Technical SEO: Created sitemap, optimized robots.txt, fixed crawl errors, and added analytics.
  • Off-Page SEO: Built backlinks and engaged in digital communities.
  • GMB Optimization: Verified listing, added keywords, posts, and optimized description and visuals.

Tools Used

  • Google Search Console & Analytics
  • Google My Business (Profile Manager)
  • Ubersuggest / Ahrefs
  • Canva (for GMB posts & visuals)
